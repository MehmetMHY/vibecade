# 3D Transcendental

## About

3D Transcendental is a thin wrapper around the interactive 3D experience hosted on [E&F Group's website](https://www.efgroupinc.com/). The page embeds the site in an iframe and adds a small toolbar with two buttons: `Refresh` to reload the scene, and `Light`/`Dark` to toggle between the site's `zen=void` (dark) and `zen=glow` (light) modes. All credit for the underlying 3D visualization belongs to [E&F Group](https://www.efgroupinc.com/). This wrapper just exists to make the experience feel at home inside Vibes Arcade alongside the original 2D [Transcendental](../transcendental/).
